\textbf{Electric Force}
The electric force is between 2 particles given by:
\begin{equation}
       \vec{E}=k \frac{qQ}{r^2}\hat{r} 
\end{equation}
where \texbf{q} and \textbf{Q} are the charges of the 2 particles
